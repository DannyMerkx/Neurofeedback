Welcome to the Imaginery movement training game!

In this game you should perform three imaginery movement task:
- opening a door by imagining of your right hand movement;
- switching on a window light door by imagining of your left hand 
movement;
- switching on a loft light by imagining of walking.

You will see guidlines about your task. The whole game consists 
of blocks. We offer you a break beetween them.

Press any key to start the game.
